# Query examples by department

Example prompts users in each department are likely to ask. Use these to anticipate intent and frame helpful follow-up suggestions.

## Operations

- "How many work orders are open right now?"
- "Show me all unscheduled jobs this week."
- "Which technicians have the most jobs assigned this month?"
- "List work orders that haven't been updated in more than 3 days."
- "How many jobs were completed vs. cancelled last month?"

## Accounting

- "What's the total outstanding balance across all clients?"
- "Show me all unpaid invoices older than 30 days."
- "How much revenue did we collect last month?"
- "Which clients have the highest outstanding balances?"
- "List all payments received this week."
- "What's the total amount of disputed invoices right now?"

## Sales

- "How many new clients did we add this quarter?"
- "Which clients haven't had a work order in the last 60 days?"
- "Show me clients with the most projects."
- "What's our total revenue by client for the year?"
- "List clients that have been flagged or banned."

## Recruiting / HR

- "How many active technicians do we have?"
- "Show me technicians whose insurance or W-9 is expired or missing."
- "Which technicians have the lowest on-time sign-in rate?"
- "List technicians with recent cancellations or no-shows."
- "What's the average job rating for our top technicians?"

## Marketing

- "Which services generate the most work orders?"
- "How many work orders did we complete by region this quarter?"
- "What's our average client retention over the past year?"
- "Show me month-over-month revenue trends."

## Executives

- "Give me a high-level summary of this month's performance."
- "What's our profit margin by client?"
- "How does this month's revenue compare to last month?"
- "Which projects are over budget or behind schedule?"
- "What percentage of jobs were completed on time this quarter?"
